Asian Lime & Cilantro

Delicioso A super simple yet super tasty way to enjoy baked, fried or grilled salmon. I'm sure it's just as good on many other types of fish as well!

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
